# Awesome-chocolates-shipment-dashboard
Title:
Awesome Chocolates: Shipment, Profit & Product Insights Dashboard
An interactive business intelligence dashboard analyzing sales, shipment distribution, product performance, and profitability to uncover actionable insights for Awesome Chocolates.

Description:
An interactive dashboard analyzing Awesome Chocolates’ sales, shipment distribution, and profitability across products, regions, and sales teams.
It highlights key KPIs, year-over-year trends, and top-performing products and salespersons.
The dashboard enables quick identification of growth opportunities and underperforming areas for data-driven decision-making.

Tech Stak:
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies.
📊 Power BI Desktop – Main tool used for building interactive dashboards and reports.

🔄 Power Query (M Language) – Data cleaning, transformation, and creating custom columns.

🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Creating measures, calculated columns, and YoY analysis.

🗂️ Data Modeling (Star Schema) – Structuring fact and dimension tables for performance optimization.

🔗 Data Relationships – Connecting multiple tables using primary and foreign keys.

📅 Calendar Table / Time Intelligence – Enabling date-based analysis and year-over-year comparisons.

📈 Data Visualization Techniques – Line charts, bar charts, donut charts, treemaps, KPI cards.

🎛️ Filters & Slicers – Interactive data filtering and user-driven analysis.

🎨 Conditional Formatting – Enhancing visuals with dynamic colors based on values.

🧩 Custom Tooltips & Report Interactions – Improving user experience and insights exploration.

🖼️ Image Integration (Image URL) – Displaying product and profile images in tables.

🧪 Report Testing & Optimization – Ensuring accuracy, performance, and usability.

🧠 UI/UX Design (Mockups & Layout Design) – Structuring dashboard for better storytelling and readability.
Data Source:
Source: Dataset acquired from a Power BI tutorial by Chandoo on YouTube, used for building and demonstrating analytics solutions.
Features and Highlights:
🚀 Features & Highlights
📌 1. Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to track sales performance, shipment efficiency, and product profitability across regions and time. Lack of a centralized dashboard makes it difficult to identify trends, top-performing products, and underperforming areas, leading to delayed or ineffective decision-making.

🎯 2. Goal of the Dashboard

The goal of this dashboard is to provide a comprehensive view of sales, profit, and shipment data by:

Enabling real-time performance tracking

Comparing current vs previous year (YoY) metrics

Identifying top products, regions, and salespersons

Improving data-driven decision-making through interactive visuals

📊 3. Walkthrough of Key Visuals

KPI Cards (Top Section)
Displays key metrics such as Total Sales ($65M), Total Boxes, Shipment Count, Total Profit, and Profit % for quick insights.

Time Series Line Charts (Amount & Boxes CY vs PY)
Shows trends over time and enables year-over-year comparison to track growth and seasonality.

Shipment Distribution (Histogram)
Visualizes shipment volume distribution to understand order patterns and frequency.

Top Products (Treemap)
Highlights best-performing products based on revenue contribution.

Salesperson Performance Table
Displays top salespersons with total sales, boxes sold, and profit %, including performance indicators.

Geographical Distribution (Donut Chart)
Shows sales contribution by region/country.

Product Performance Table
Detailed view of product-wise revenue and profit %, enhanced with conditional formatting.

Slicers & Filters (Top Right)
Allows users to dynamically filter data by date range and other dimensions.

💡 4. Business Impact & Insights

Identified top-performing products contributing the highest revenue, enabling focused marketing strategies.

Highlighted loss-making or low-profit products, helping stakeholders take corrective actions.

Enabled tracking of sales trends and seasonal patterns, improving forecasting accuracy.

Provided visibility into regional performance, supporting expansion and optimization decisions.

Helped evaluate salesperson efficiency, improving performance management.

Reduced manual reporting efforts by delivering a fully interactive and automated dashboard.
